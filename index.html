About

Activist, Coach, Mentor, Mother, Wife, and Friend

Thushyanthi Muruges, ACC, is an Equitable Design & Impact Lead at Culture Amp, based in Austin, Texas. With a passion for fostering career happiness and designing equitable systems, Thushyanthi is dedicated to creating inclusive environments where everyone can thrive.

At Culture Amp, Thushyanthi serves as the Equitable Design & Impact Lead within the People & Experience team, reporting directly to the VP of Equitable Operations. In this role, she plays a pivotal part in operationalizing Culture Amp's Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) strategy. Her responsibilities include developing and formalizing Employee Resource Groups (ERGs), producing equitable data reports, and conducting EDI audits to ensure fairness in performance evaluations, pay, and promotions.

Thushyanthi is certified as an Associate Certified Coach (ACC) and a Professional Certified Coach (PCC), reflecting her expertise in coaching and talent strategy. With a multilingual background encompassing Tamil, French, and English, she brings a diverse perspective to her work.

Prior to her current role, Thushyanthi held various positions where she contributed to DEI efforts and talent development initiatives. At Insight Data Science, she served as a Consultant and later as the Head of Labs Operations and DEI Lead, focusing on diversifying the data science workplace and managing DEI subgroups. She also held roles at Shift, Perspective, and Insight Data Science, where she contributed to talent development, strategic partnerships, and program management.

Thushyanthi holds a Master of Arts degree in International Development and Social Change from Clark University and a Bachelor of Arts in French Studies from Berry College. With a rich background spanning diverse industries and a commitment to equity and inclusion, Thushyanthi is dedicated to making meaningful contributions to creating fair and inclusive workplaces.
